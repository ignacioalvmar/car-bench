Competition Registration

Register through the Google Form if possible. If Google Forms is inaccessible from your location, use the email fallback below.

Register via Google Form Email fallback
The Google Form is the preferred registration path because it helps organizers process teams quickly. The email fallback collects the same information and is intended for participants who cannot access Google Forms, including participants in regions where Google services may be unavailable.
Preferred

Google Form

Use this path if the form opens for you. It is the fastest way to register and gives organizers a structured response.

Email Fallback

If Google Forms is inaccessible, send the information listed below by email. Please use the subject line CAR-bench Competition Registration.

View email instructions

Required Information

  • Team name
  • Institution or organization
  • Country or region
  • Contact person name
  • Contact email
  • Team member names
  • Team member emails, optional
  • Track selection: Track 1 only, Track 2 only, or both

Track 2 Information

Teams registering for Track 2 must also provide:

  • One ChatGPT-account-linked email for Codex Pro access
  • If a private ChatGPT account should not be shared with the team, create a dedicated team account before registering
  • A short, non-binding description of the intended submission or innovation idea
  • Confirmation that only one Codex Pro subscription can be provided per team
  • Confirmation that teams receiving Codex Pro access are strongly expected to actively participate and submit a final entry

Track 2 participation is limited to 15 teams. The short innovation idea may be used to select participating teams if more than 15 teams register.
