Organizers

A multidisciplinary team spanning academia and industry, united by the goal of building reliable AI agents.

Lead Organizer
Johannes Kirmayr
PhD Candidate, BMW Group Research & Augsburg University, Germany
Lead author of CAR-bench. Accepted at ACL 2026 Main. 1st place at UC Berkeley’s AgentX-AgentBeats Competition (Google DeepMind-sponsored). Hugging Face Paper of the Day.
Lead Organizer
Lukas Stappen
Research Scientist, BMW Group Research, Germany
Working on LLM-based voice assistants. PhD in human-centric multimodal learning. Founded the MuSe Challenge series (2020–2026) at ACM MM (A*). Data chair at Interspeech ComParE. PC member at IJCAI/ECAI. >1,600 citations, h-index 19.
Lead Organizer
Elisabeth André
Full Professor, Augsburg University, Germany
Chair of Human-Centered AI. Leibniz Prize recipient. Fellow of EurAI, member of Leopoldina and CHI Academy. Former EiC of IEEE TAC. Co-organizer of the MultiMediate Grand Challenge at ACM Multimedia (2021–2026). >27,000 citations, h-index 77.
Industry
Patrick Dreisch
GTM, Anthropic, Germany
Founding member of Anthropic’s Go-To-Market team for DACH. Ex-Google, ex-AWS, ex-Amazon. Anthropic is the AI company behind Claude, the top-performing model on CAR-bench.
Industry
Natalia Vassilieva
VP & Field CTO ML, Cerebras Systems, USA
PhD in CS. decades of R&D experience in NLP, CV, ML and IR. Previously Sr. Research Manager at HP Labs. Cerebras builds the world’s fastest AI inference infrastructure, holding independently verified speed records on frontier-scale models.
Industry
Raj Tumuluri
CEO, OpenStream.ai, USA
Multimodal conversational AI pioneer. Co-author of W3C standards on multimodal, speech, and automotive web. Co-chaired several IEEE/ACM conferences on multimodal interaction. OpenStream.ai builds enterprise agentic AI systems focused on trustworthy, hallucination-free outcomes grounded in domain knowledge.
Academia
Erik Cambria
Full Professor, NTU Singapore; Visiting Professor, MIT Media Lab
IEEE Fellow and Clarivate Top 1% Scientist. Founder of SenticNet. Co-organizer of MuSe Challenge and ACM MM 2025 Grand Challenge. Featured in Forbes as one of the 5 People Building Our AI Future. >84,000 citations, h-index 132.
Academia
Iryna Gurevych
Full Professor, TU Darmstadt, Germany
Head of UKP Lab. Past ACL President (2023). First Recipient of the ATHENE Distinguished Professorship. Royal Society Milner Award (2025), member of Academia Europaea and Leopoldina, Co-Director of the ELLIS NLP program. >66,000 citations, h-index 98.
Academia
Varin Sikka
Researcher, Stanford University, USA
Co-author of “Hallucination Stations,” a theoretical complexity framework for LLM hallucination limitations. International Linguistics Olympiad Gold medalist.
