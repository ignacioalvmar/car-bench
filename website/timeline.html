Timeline

Key dates and deadlines for the CAR-bench Challenge.

All times are in AoE (Anywhere on Earth, UTC−12). When a deadline shifts, we will strikethrough the old date and show the new one.
April 2026
Competition Opens
Data, documentation, baseline agent, and Docker template released. Registration opens. Dev-set evaluation available for local testing.
We are here
May – July 2026
Open Development Phase
Participants develop agents and submit to the live dev-set leaderboard. Continuous submissions accepted. Iterate, improve, and climb the leaderboard.
July 19, 2026
Final Evaluation on Hidden Test Set
Your agent runs against the hidden test set. This is your final score. Rankings are determined from this round.
Hidden test set · Final rankings
July 26, 2026
Technical Report Deadline
Submit a 4-page technical report (IJCAI format) describing your approach. Required for award eligibility. Selected reports published as non-archival proceedings.
4 pages · IJCAI format
July 31, 2026
Winners Announced
Open Track rank awards (1st–3rd), Best Innovation Award, and Cerebras Innovation Award announced. Certificates and prize details communicated to winners.
August 15–21, 2026
Presentations at IJCAI-ECAI 2026, Bremen
3-hour dedicated session: welcome and overview (30 min), oral presentations by top teams (90 min), panel discussion on LLM agent reliability (30 min), awards and closing (30 min).
Bremen, Germany
